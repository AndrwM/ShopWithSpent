About

Mission

To deliver relevance in everyday life.

Gone are the days of boring circulars and TV/radio ads. If you want to compete, you need to focus on digital personalization.
Top 2 ways consumers make purchasing decisions:
1 - Friends & family recommendations
2 - Public recommendations
88% of retailers say that "deeper customer engagement to drive sales thru personalized offers" was a very valuable opportunity with mobile.

Shopper marketing can be better, and consumer’s lives could be made easier. No more generic advertisements, no more paper coupons. Spent delivers what matters most to people, based on what they buy. And it arrives when they need it, and how they want it. Bottom line, consumers deserve a better, personal experience, and relevance brings just that. Plus, we like to save trees.

The Spent platform uses purchase history and shopper behavior to helps retailers and brands talk directly to their customers, automatically. We replace untrusted, irrelevant advertisements with trusted, personalized recommendations that generate more loyalty, and more sales. And, we make product discovery personal, fun and relevant to each individual. Whether you have a small shop, or you’re a large chain with multiple stores, Spent’s personalized recommendations can improve your digital store marketing.

We could tell you about us all day long, but the easiest way to reveal the power of Spent is to show you. Contact us to get started.
